What we do

We deal in a broad spectrum of refrigeration business. Given below are some major areas of interest.

Domestic Refrigeration

Acrylic/plastic spares, evaporators, condensors, compressors, thermostats, timers, bi-metals and more.

Commercial Refrigeration

Reciprocating compressors, condensors, evaporators, motors, controllers, fans and more.

Industrial Refrigeration

Scroll compressors, fan-coil units, receivers, accumulators, oil separators, valves, condensors, fans, controllers and more.

Consumables

Refrigerants, butane cartridges, PU foam spray, coil cleaners, brazing flux & alloy, tapes, leak locators, coatings and more.

Tools & Accessories

Vacuum pumps, tube cutters, flaring tools, gauge manifolds, charging hoses, ratchets, pinch-off tools, tool kits, power tools, hardware tools and more.
